Definition

The Focal Point is the most important thing in the picture, it is the center of attention that the eye is drawn towards (Hagen and Golombisky 53).

In Summary

The Focal Point is what you want the viewer to pay attention to. Generally it should utilize 2/3 of the image and there should be one per image.

Real World Context

So whenever you are taking a picture or making some kind of distributable flier, keep in mind what you would like people to focus on. If you have more than one focal point the image or page will become clustered and messy.
