Definition

The perception that things that are alike are in the same group (Hagen and Golombisky 62).

In Summary

If you put two different things in the same group they will be mentally organized based on the what looks the same. If you have a pile of Skittles, and you add a some Reese's Pieces into the middle, it'll be perceived as a pile of Reese's within a pile of Skittle rather than one pile of candy.

Real World Context

Keep alike things together, don't try and make a paragraph or photo set that have no relation to each other. Find the things that are similar and arrange them in a way that the consumer can see the connection.
