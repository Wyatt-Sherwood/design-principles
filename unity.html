Definition

All parts of a design work together and feel like they belong in that design (Hagen and Golombisky 60).

In Summary

Kinda like what I said the last one this is how everything ties together and fits. Looking at the bigger picture of a design, Unity is the principle that makes sure that things don't clash and everything feels one singular product.

Real World Context

Unity will naturally occur to your work if you stick to these basic principles. Because in the end, unity just means that you made a layout with elements that work together and create a flow that makes the whole project better.
