Definition

The inclusion of two differing elements to avoid boredom on a page (Hagen and Golombisky 56).

In Summary

There are lots of ways to add contrast, whether it's in colors or font style. Contrasting is what will make the page interesting rather than a monotonous blob.

Real World Context

Add contrasting elements to your projects in the future, they create more interesting and dynamic layouts. Mix up your use of negative space and choose a color pallet that makes both colors stand out through contrast.
