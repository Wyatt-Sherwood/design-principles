Definition

Keeping the visual weight of the page equal. The three kinds of balance are radial, symmetrical, and asymmetrical (Hagen and Golombisky 56)

In Summary

You want the page to look balanced, you can't just throw up pictures and text at random. There has to be some order to it, and a balanced page is the way to creating a pleasing page layout.

Real World Context

When trying to make a layout balanced, don't be afraid to try making it asymmetrical. Balance in design all comes down to visual weight, and visual weight can be shifted by moving the center around.
